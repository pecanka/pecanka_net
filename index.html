About Us
The reason I started a statistical consulting company is simple. In one sentence, I wanted to make a direct and immediate impact. Having spent over a decade in academic environments around Europe focusing on both consulting work and research in the areas of biostatistics, statistical genetics and medical statistics, I came to the conclusion that I wanted to set off on a new path. A path that would be simultaneously broader and more focused. A path that would allow me to utilize my statistical expertise in a wide variety of applied contexts both inside and outside the world of academic research, but also one that would allow me to focus towards using data to help clients make a difference in their endeavors in the fast-paced, outcome oriented, merit rewarding world that we live in. Achieving these goals is the purpose of this company.
Jakub Pecanka, PhD
founder and chief analyst
